About Nova

Nova 75 Capsule 10's belongs to a group of medications called 'anti-convulsant' primarily used in the prevention of neuropathic pain, epilepsy (seizures episodes), fibromyalgia (musculoskeletal pain), and neuralgia (pain due to damaged/irritated nerve). Neuropathic pain is a chronic nerve pain usually caused by damaged nerves due to a variety of diseases like diabetes, shingles (viral infection that causes a painful rash), or spinal cord injury, injuries to tissue, muscles, or joints.

Nova 50mg Capsule 10's contains 'Pregabalin' that works by calming the damaged or overactive nerves in your body that may cause nerve or musculoskeletal pain or seizures. Besides this, it also decreases the number of pain signals sent out by damaged nerves in the body.

Nova 50mg Capsule 10's can be taken with or without a meal with a full glass of water. A person taking Nova 50mg Capsule 10's might experience side effects like dizziness, somnolence (sleepiness/drowsiness), dry mouth, oedema (fluid overload with swelling), blurred vision, weight gain and thinking abnormal (primarily difficulty with concentration/attention). Although, not every person faces the side effects of Nova 50mg Capsule 10's, and even some of the unpleasant effects of Nova 50mg Capsule 10's do not require medical attention.
